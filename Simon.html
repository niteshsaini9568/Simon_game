How to Play:

  • Game Start: To start a new game, press the "Start" or "New Game" button.
  • Game Sequence: Simon will play a sequence of colors and sounds. Pay close attention to the sequence, as you'll need to repeat it later.
  • Your Turn: After Simon's sequence, it's your turn. Use the colored buttons (usually four) to repeat the sequence in the exact same order.
    • Click or tap the buttons to repeat the sequence. For example, if Simon played Red, Green, Blue, you should click the corresponding buttons in the same order.
    1. Correct Sequence: If you correctly repeat the sequence, Simon will add another step to the sequence, making it one step longer.
    2. Incorrect Sequence: If you make a mistake and don't repeat the sequence correctly, the game will end, and your score will be displayed.
    3. Winning: The goal is to reach the highest level and the longest sequence possible. Challenge yourself to see how far you can go!
